Summary of Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to analyze and visualize the weather trends of more than 500 cities around the world. To complete this analysis, I used the OpenWeatherMap API to generate the data of more than 500 cities. Next, I used pandas to clean and assemble the dataset. Finally, I used MatPlotLib to plot the scatter plots. The relationships I analyzed for correlation were the city's latitude vs. cloudiness, humidity, max temperature, and windspeed.
