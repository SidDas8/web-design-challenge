Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Cities close to a latitude of zero tend to have significantly higher chances of cloudiness. In addition, cities with a latitude of -40 to -20 and 20 to 50, have a much lower chance for cloudiness. The correlation between latitude and cloudiness appears to be weak.
